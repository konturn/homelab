# CPK US (cpkus.com)

**CPK Construction, Inc.** is the Kontur family construction business founded in 1989 by Chris and Patty Kontur in Northfield, Ohio.

## Business Overview
- **Founded:** 1989 as small carpentry contracting firm
- **Incorporated:** 1996 as general contracting company  
- **Location:** Northfield, Ohio 44067
- **Contact:** 330-467-5918, info@cpkus.com
- **Website:** cpkus.com

## Services
- Residential and commercial architectural design-build
- Custom carpentry and cabinet making
- General contracting
- Basement and bathroom remodeling
- Custom home construction
- Historical restoration and preservation
- Energy-efficient and sustainable building

## Family Business
True family business with both founders' sons now involved:
- **Chris Kontur** - Founder, President (custom carpentry expertise)
- **Patty Kontur** - Co-founder (business operations)
- **Evan Kontur** - Son, involved in business operations
- **Jonathan Kontur** - Son, part of CPK team

The company emphasizes integrity, quality craftsmanship, and fair pricing while maintaining family values in their operations.
